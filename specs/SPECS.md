# Project Specs

## What We Are Building

PDF-Fasi is a lightweight desktop application for working with PDF files.
It targets Windows first, while remaining compatible with macOS.
The app is intended for users who need simple PDF operations and prefer a local
desktop tool over online services.

## Why

Users need a fast, usable, and trustworthy way to perform common PDF tasks
without uploading files to third-party services.
The product aims to keep the experience simple while staying modern and
accessible.

## How

The product will start with compression as the primary feature.
Additional capabilities such as merge/split, conversion to and from other
formats, editable PDF workflows, and simple in-app editing are considered
future features and should be introduced iteratively.
The UI should stay very simple, attractive, modern, and accessible.

## Non-Goals

The initial scope does not include building every PDF feature at once.
The product is not intended to become a complex all-in-one PDF suite in the
first release.
Online processing services are out of scope.

## Operating Principles

Focus on the core value first: compression.
Keep the application lightweight, fast, and easy to use.
Prefer incremental delivery and Agile-style prioritization for future features.
Design for usability first, then expand functionality step by step.
